What we do

At the Brooklyn College Computer Science Club we promote Computer Science and hacker culture at Brooklyn College. Our goal is to cultivate maker creativity among the students on campus. We’re currently organizing technical workshops for all kinds of people, from beginner to advance. During club hours, you would be able to swing by and talk about tech, ask for help with a CS program, and meet like-minded people.

We're estatic and looking forward to running fun and informative events throughout the year!
